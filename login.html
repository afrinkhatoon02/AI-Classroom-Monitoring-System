AI BASED SMART CLASSROOM

Overview

Modern classrooms face growing challenges with student distractions such as phone usage, sleeping, talking, and lack of attention. An AI-based intelligent monitoring system detects these activities, provides real-time feedback, tracks attendance, and helps teachers maintain student engagement effectively.

Mission

Our mission is to create a smart classroom environment where technology supports teaching by:

  • Automatically tracking student attendance.
  • Detecting sleeping, phone usage, and talking during class.
  • Monitoring student attention and engagement.
  • Generating detailed reports for teachers to improve learning outcomes.
